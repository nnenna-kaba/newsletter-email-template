All about the door

Dear Mr. Zimmermann,

For some time now the trade press has been reporting that arches are making a comeback in architecture. dormakaba has taken this development into account and developed the new flat rocker arm for doors with segmental or round arches, the use of which enables the use of the BTS 80 system floor spring on these unusual doors. There is also news from other areas: we have modernized the function for controlling barrier-free toilet doors on the ED 100/250, launched a new sustainability program at dormakaba and expanded our knowledge database #weknowhow to include topics such as burglary protection. And last but not least, we take up the topic of visualization, which is becoming increasingly important as a sales-support measure in the digital age. We hope you enjoy reading.

Your editorial team at dormakaba Deutschland GmbH

Flat rocker arm with BTS 80 systems: the special solution for unusual 1 and 2-leaf doors

On segment or arched doors with load-bearing hinges, it is often technically impossible to install an overhead door closer. In order not to do without the convenience of a door closer, however, the solution in many cases is to combine the BTS 80 floor spring with a so-called flat rocker arm, which ensures that the force is transmitted from the floor spring to the door leaf.

Whether 1-leaf or 2-leaf, whether with a milled or attached rail, whether on the hinge or hinge side, DIN left or DIN right: with its numerous variants, the flat rocker arm is suitable for a wide variety of installation situations. With an opening angle of almost 180 on the hinge-side variant, it also enables a high level of convenience, while its stainless-steel-colored finish underscores the high-quality design of special doors.

We have summarized the technical details of the new flat rocker arm for you in our new fact sheet. The corresponding drawings in DWG format can be found in the download area of our homepage
